Koi (A story about what Love represents)

By Sy'rae Ocasio

Koi is a heart that is searching for something that he cannot find, nor understand what it is. Until one day he stumbles upon something he never knew he really wanted in his life...

Koi endlessly searching the abyss for something he cannot describe but feels he needs

He finds something encased in a glass box, eminating power within him

The desire to obtain this power is so much that Koi begins to smash the glass to have it

Koi is frantically smashing the glass to have the "Love" power, the glass bursts on himself

After severely hurting himself, Koi realises that this power is an entity with it's own free will

Love materializes into a woman, which leaves Koi extremely confused. Find out what happens in Part 2...

Koi's Design, he's fairly simple so that it's not a hassle to draw him

Love's design is flowy and beautiful because I feel love would be a beautiful person if the feeling itself were human
